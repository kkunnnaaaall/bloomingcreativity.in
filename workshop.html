Moonlight & Mosaic Art Workshop @ Avadh Uthopia, Vapi | Berry & Lush Exhibition

Experience a magical evening of creativity at our Moonlight & Mosaic Art Workshop! Held at the stunning Avadh Uthopia as part of the Berry & Lush Exhibition, this workshop invites you to unwind, explore your artistic side, and create your own mosaic masterpiece under the stars. All materials provided. No prior experience needed just bring your imagination!

🎨 Art Corner | Chickoo Festival, Bordi Beach 🍃✨

Nestled under the gentle shade of lush orchards by the breezy Bordi Beach, the Art Corner became a colourful escape for visitors of all ages at the Chickoo Festival. From curious little ones splashing their first strokes of colour to seniors reviving their love for painting, the space buzzed with laughter, creativity, and pure joy. Families sat together on mats, brushes and palettes in hand, painting vibrant memories inspired by nature’s calm and the beach’s salty breeze. The Art Corner reminded everyone that sometimes, all you need is a brush, a splash of colour, and a bit of seaside sunshine to create smiles that last forever.  🌿🎉

🎨 Live Customise Station | Flea Market, Vapi ✨

Here’s a cheerful glimpse into our buzzing Live Customise Station at the Vapi Flea Market! Visitors of all ages had a blast personalising caps and goggles, designing quirky jelly duffel bags, and crafting their own pencil zipper boxes. It was amazing to see little ones and grown-ups alike dive into colours and creativity, turning simple items into special keepsakes. Every smile, every unique design, and every proud “I made this!” moment made it truly special. Huge thanks to everyone who joined in the fun — your love keeps our creative spark alive!🫶🏻✨
